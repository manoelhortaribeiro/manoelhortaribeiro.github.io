I'm an Assistant Professor at Princeton University in the Department of Computer Science, affiliated with the Princeton Center for Information Technology Policy (CITP) and Princeton HCI.

At Princeton, I lead the Humans and Machines Lab. Our research seeks to understand, design, and improve sociotechnical systems in the age of generative AI. I like research that tackles real-world issues and provides actionable insights. Ideally, these insights translate into policy and design changes.

I am not accepting new PhD students at this time.
