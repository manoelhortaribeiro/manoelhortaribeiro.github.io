👤bio

I'm a 2nd year PhD student at EPFL🇨🇭 working at dlab with Robert West. My research uses a diverse methodological toolkit to characterize troublesome online phenomena and to assess how moderation policies and recommendation algorithms can be leveraged to improve our online information ecosystem. I received my B.S./M.S. in Computer Science from UFMG🇧🇷, where I worked with Wagner Meira Jr. and Virgílio Almeida. I'm also a member of iDrama🎭.
